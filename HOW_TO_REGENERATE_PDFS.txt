IF SOMETHING LOOKS WRONG
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Problem: Personal playbook still shows old values or neighborhood analysis

Solution:
  1. Hard refresh the page (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows)
  2. Clear your browser cache
  3. Try in a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
  4. Contact venkatesan.ps@gmail.com with screenshot if issue persists

Problem: App shows "Probably not worth protesting this year" instead of
         green "Bottom Line" box

Solution:
  This means the code hasn't updated yet. The GitHub Actions deploy takes
  ~2 minutes after code was pushed. Wait 5 minutes, then refresh.

FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Will the app always show $808,789 as the request?
A: Yes. The 3 Angel Falls Dr comps have median $241.87/sqft, which yields
   $808,789. This is consistent and defensible.

Q: What if DCAD adds more comps to their analysis?
A: The statute allows "a reasonable number." 3 same-street comps is clearly
   reasonable. Anything they do to defend their position gives you more
   evidence of inconsistency to cite in the hearing.

Q: Should I use the app's numbers or calculate them myself?
A: Use the app's numbers. They're pulled live from Denton CAD and are
   verifiable. DCAD can't argue with their own data.

Q: Can I print just one copy of the Board Packet?
A: No. You must provide one to each of the 3 ARB members + 1 for yourself.
   4 copies total. Print on good quality paper (color if possible).

Q: Do I need the Redfin ZIP trend data in the Board Packet?
A: It's useful context (shows ZIP 75036 median sale is $546K, far below
   your appraised $836K, proving overvaluation). Leave it in.
